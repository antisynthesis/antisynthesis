Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Overview

ANTISYNTHESIS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection for our applications and services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of our applications. Our apps are designed to function entirely on your device without requiring any data transmission to our servers.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contact information
  • Financial information

Data Storage

Any data generated or stored by our applications remains solely on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor do we transmit it to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@antisynthesis.com.
